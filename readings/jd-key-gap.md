# Key Gap from Job Description

## Role: BI Manager

## The Responsibility That's New to Me

> "Investor Relations exposure and/or hands-on experience supporting fundraising processes, including due diligence process, is a strong plus."

Job Description

- Design, build, and continuously refine executive dashboards covering Revenue & TPV, P&L metrics, cloud costs, and other operational metrics. 
- Establish and enforce a single source of truth across Finance, BD, Product, Operations, and Risk. 
- Define and document metric logic, calculation methodologies, and governance standards to ensure consistency and clarity across the organization. 
- Ensure alignment between management reporting and financial/statutory views in partnership with Finance. 
- Align cross-functional stakeholders on metric ownership and accountability. 
- Translate complex datasets into clear narratives and actionable recommendations for leadership. 
- Partner with Data Engineering to strengthen data integrity, improve automation, and enhance reporting reliability. 

Requirements
- 4–7 years of experience in Business Intelligence and Analytics. 
- Demonstrated ownership of cross-functional executive dashboards. 
- Strong SQL proficiency and hands-on experience with BI tools (Metapods, Looker, Power BI, etc.) 
- Solid understanding of unit economics, cohort analysis, and P&L dynamics. 
- Experience presenting structured insights to senior stakeholders. 
- Background in fintech, payments, marketplace, or high-growth tech environments preferred. 
- Comfortable operating independently in a fast-scaling, ambiguous environment. 
- Investor Relations exposure and/or hands-on experience supporting fundraising processes, including due diligence process, is a strong plus.
